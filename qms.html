QUALITY MANAGEMENT SYSTEMS

Why QMS is Required:

  1. This is a tool to make over the face of a pharma company to enter into regulatory market.
  2. Marketing get edge over its competitors
  3. Selling Price goes up like anything (manifolds)
  4. Minimizes the losses in manufacturing by increasing the efficiency, reducing the rejections.
  5. Enhances the level of discipline and traceability.








How we work on QMS:

” We Design, Rationalize and Integrate QMS”

  1. Study the Existing System and perform the gap analysis for current practices vis-a-vis expectations from regulatory agencies.
  2. Provide solutions to bridge the gaps
  3. Help out the clients to have the Quality Systems in-line with ICH-Quality Guidelines, WHO-TRS, USFDA-21 CFR
  4. Provides tools for continuous review and upgrade the Quality Systems
