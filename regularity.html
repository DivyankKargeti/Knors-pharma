Regulatory Affairs




Regulatory Affairs is a driving section of pharma industry, which takes the company into the regulated market. Our Regulatory team have experience of compilation and filing of dossiers in regulatory agencies across the world We provide complete regulatory solution to the pharma companies by facilitating in:

>> Compilation of Dossiers:
- DMFs (For APIs and Excipients)- AP and RP As per CTD and ACTD
- APIMFs (WHO-Geneva)
- ASMF (Europe)
- JP-DMF
- CEP (EDQM)
- ANDA (Abbreviated New Drug Application)
- COPP (Certificate of Pharmaceutical Products) – CDSCO
- WC (Written Confirmation) – for export Pharmaceutical products to Europe from India
- Facilitating to file the dossiers to regulatory authorities
- Inspection of manufacturing Facilities in-line with requirements of USFDA, WHO-Geneva, EDQM to identify the gaps and relate CAPA
- Compilation of summary of product characteristics (SMPC), PIL and package inserts for generics and FDC.
- Pharmacovigilance Services:
- Preparation and review of Periodic Safety Update Reports (PSUR) and Periodic Benefit Risk Evaluation Reports (PBRER)
- Compilation of clinical and non-clinical summaries
- Medical writing
