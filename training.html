ABOUT TRAININGS



Good Manufacturing Practice (GMP) is a system of practices for pharmaceutical manufacturing which ensures that products are meeting its quality standards consistently. GMP covers all aspects of production from the starting materials, premises and equipment to the training and personal hygiene of staff. Training of the personnel is an important tool for implementing cGMP.


                We provide training sessions on:


     1. cGMP in-line with ICHQ7, WHO-TRS 986, UFDA Guideline
     2.Root cause Analysis
     3.Corrective and Preventive Actions (CAPA)
     4.Risk Management as per ICH Q9
     5.Investigations (For OOS/OOT/Deviations/Complaints)
     6.Internal Audits (Self Inspections) and Certification of Inspectors
     7.Data Integrity
     8.Change Management
     9.Audits and Compliances
     10.Handling of Regulatory Audits
     11.Development of Quality Culture
