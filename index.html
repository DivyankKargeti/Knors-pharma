About Us

We KNORS Pharma Solutions is a Limited Liability Partnership Company registered in Ministry of Corporate Affairs Government of India. We are a team of experts in the field of Design and Implementation of Quality Management Systems, Regulatory Affairs, GMP Trainings, GMP Inspections and Compliances. Our team has vast experience in Pharmaceutical Manufacturing and regulatory compliances. Having the versatility of experiences we provide integrated solutions to pharmaceutical companies for their compliance with regulatory requirements. There are generalized quality standards for the pharmaceutical industry, but the way to achieve these standards varies from region to region, process to process, company to company and one culture to another. We study such variables and accordingly design the quality systems for pharmaceutical companies.

DIRECTOR-Dr. Rajesh Kumar Kaushik

Our Director Dr. Rajesh Kumar Kaushik hast vast and versatile experience of the pharmaceutical industry. He is a qualified lead auditor from IRCA Certified Course, RSSL United Kingdom. He is also a qualified auditor for inspecting finished pharmaceutical products, Active Pharmaceutical Ingredients (API), intermediates, raw materials, packaging materials, excipients and testing laboratories. His versatility comprises of Quality Assurance, Quality Control, Analytical Development, IPR, Manufacturing and Regulatory Affairs. He has experiences of transforming non-regulated companies into fully regulated companies with certifications from USFDA, WHO-Geneva, EU-GMP, EDQM, MHRA, and MFDS-Korea. His forte is to rationalize the quality systems in-line with regulatory guidelines, regulatory requirements and are feasible to comply. He trains the teams in a way that a quality culture is developed in the company and hence quality compliments the process and business.
